We don't just breed dogs.
We raise family members.

Cali Doodles started in our living room — literally. When our first Aussie Doodle pup changed our family, we knew we wanted to share that joy with others throughout California and beyond.

Every litter is raised in our home with our children, our routines, and our chaos. Puppies leave us fully socialized to sounds, surfaces, strangers, and the beautiful unpredictability of family life.

We health test all of our breeding dogs through OFA and Embark DNA, share results openly, and match each puppy carefully to the right home — not just the next applicant.

How it works

Simple, transparent, and personal. No auctions, no bidding. Just good people finding the right dog.

  1. 1
    Submit an inquiry

    Fill out our short contact form. Tell us about your home, lifestyle, and what you're looking for.

  2. 2
    We get in touch

    We'll schedule a quick call to answer your questions and make sure we're the right fit for each other.

  3. 3
    Reserve your puppy

    A $500 deposit holds your place. We send weekly photo and video updates right up to pick-up day.

  4. 4
    Welcome home

    Pick-up at 8 weeks. Your puppy comes with a vet check, first vaccinations, a health guarantee, and a puppy packet.
